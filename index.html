The Tiger

About Tigers

The tiger (Pantherna Trigis) is the world's most biggest cat species,and mostly recognizable for the pattern on the black striped stripes on their orange fur.The other animals in the big cat family are lions,leopards jaguar,and snow leopards.
Tigers are the meridian of predators.They hunt mammals like deers and bovids.Tigers are endangered animals because many people like to hunt tigers for their skin.
